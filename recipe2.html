Patatas Bravas

This creamy, herb-infused feta dip is the perfect appetizer for any occasion. It combines the tangy flavor of feta with smooth cream cheese, accented by a blend of fresh herbs like parsley, dill, and chives. The result is a deliciously creamy and savory spread that pairs wonderfully with bread, crackers, or fresh veggies. Easy to prepare and full of flavor, this dip is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.


INGREDIENTS

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (adjust to desired spice level)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

INSTRUCTIONS

STEP 1: Heat a pot of salted water and bring it to a boil. Add the cubed potatoes and cook for 5â€“7 minutes until just tender. Drain well and pat dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture. Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, fry the potatoes in batches until crispy and golden brown (about 5-7 minutes per batch). Remove and drain on paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.

STEP 2: In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft and translucent (about 5 minutes). Add the minced garlic, smoked paprika, and chili flakes. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant. Add the tomato paste and crushed tomatoes. Simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt, pepper, and white wine vinegar to balance the flavors.

STEP 3: Arrange the crispy potatoes on a serving dish. Spoon the bravas sauce generously over the potatoes. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve immediately. Enjoy with a side of aioli or your favorite dipping sauce.

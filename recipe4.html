Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad is a classic Italian dish that embodies the fresh, vibrant flavors of summer. It is a light, yet satisfying combination of ripe, juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves, all brought together with a simple drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil. Often served as an appetizer or a refreshing side dish, this salad is a celebration of the Mediterranean diet and is perfect for any occasion.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
    • 250g (9 oz) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2-3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Optional: 1 tbsp balsamic glaze

INSTRUCTIONS

STEP 1: On a serving plate, alternate slices of tomato, mozzarella, and basil leaves in an overlapping pattern.

STEP 2: Drizzle olive oil over the salad. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.

STEP 3: Drizzle with balsamic glaze for added sweetness and tang.

STEP 4: Serve immediately as a fresh and vibrant appetizer or side dish.

APPETIZERS

Bruschetta al Pomodoro

A tasty Italian snack made with toasted bread topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, olive oil, and garlic. It's simple, fresh, and full of flavor!

Patatas Bravas

Patatas Bravas is a classic Spanish tapas dish made of crispy fried potatoes served with a spicy tomato sauce.

Whipped Feta with Herbs

Whipped Feta with Herbs is a creamy, tangy dip infused with fresh herbs, perfect for pairing with crackers, pita, or fresh veggies.

Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad is a simple and refreshing Italian dish made with ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, fresh basil, and a drizzle of olive oil

ENTREES

Ravioli di Ricotta e Spinaci

Ravioli di Ricotta e Spinaci is a traditional Italian dish featuring tender pasta filled with creamy ricotta cheese and sautéed spinach, often served with a simple butter or tomato sauce.

Coq au Vin

Coq au Vin is a classic French dish of chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, onions, and bacon, resulting in a rich and flavorful stew.

Paella Valenciana

Paella Valenciana is a traditional Spanish dish from Valencia, made with a combination of chicken, rabbit, green beans, and saffron-infused rice cooked to perfection in a wide, shallow pan.

Sauerbraten

Sauerbraten is a traditional German pot roast, typically made with beef marinated in a tangy mix of vinegar, wine, and spices, then slow-cooked until tender. It’s often served with red cabbage, potato dumplings, or boiled potatoes.

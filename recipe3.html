Whipped Feta with Herbs

A tasty Italian snack made with toasted bread topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, olive oil, and garlic. It's simple, fresh, and full of flavor!


INGREDIENTS

  • 200g (7 oz) feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese (softened)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (plus extra for drizzling)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Zest of 1 lemon

INSTRUCTIONS

STEP 1: In a food processor, combine feta cheese and cream cheese. Blend until smooth and creamy.

STEP 2: Add olive oil, garlic, parsley, dill, and chives. Blend again until herbs are evenly incorporated.

STEP 3: Taste and season with salt, pepper, and lemon zest if desired.

STEP 4: Transfer to a serving bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and garnish with extra herbs. Serve immediately with bread, crackers, or veggies, or refrigerate until ready to serve.
